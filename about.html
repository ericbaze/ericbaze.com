People call me a hybrid, a unicorn, a generalist, a jack of all trades. I'm really much more.

I have 14 years of experience creating and managing all aspects of online experiences. I specialize in visual design, user experience, and front-end development within a marketing and electronic commerce context for desktop and mobile device delivery.

Secondary skills related to this include information architecture, content strategy, social media, back-end development, and server administration. I also have experience in print design, photography, and digital audio/video production.

From researching and recommending technology stacks to brand positioning, email marketing, event promotion and CRM -- I've had a hand in it. I won't pretend I'm an expert at everything, but I know when to ask, when to read, and when to outsource. I see the big picture, and I understand all the moving parts.

As the industry matures, I am very interested in taking on projects to both focus on and enhance my visual design and user experience skills while maintaining technical proficiency -- particularly in SaaS and product development.

My Specialties

Visual Design

I have two goals as a designer: create beautiful, useful things and be a better designer. Having worked in small and large companies, private industry and non-profit, I've had the opportunity to design a wide variety of print and online materials. The best part? Creating strong products and brands with a consistent voice.

User Experience

Creating seductive, delightful, and engaging experiences is at the core of what I do, whether it be online or not. I always say that one can engineer the most robust product in the world, but if people don't like using it, it's a failure. I'm passionate about helping companies keep people at the core of their business while creating successful products.

Front-End Development

As with any designer, photographer or artist, there are tools and techniques that each use to produce their work. The multiple flavors of HTML, CSS, and Javascript are the substrate for which I create mine. Writing responsive, accessible, standards-based code is my Zen activity. I also love making my process more efficient with solid frameworks like Bootstrap.

Content Strategy

For 10+ years I've been writing and editing content professionally for a variety of print and online distribution channels, providing a strategic methodology across platforms. Though specializing in content for marketing, e-commerce and instruction, I'm no stranger to the impact of content strategy on the user experience. I keep the AP Style Guide app on hand!
